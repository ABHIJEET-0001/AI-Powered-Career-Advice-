# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

AI Career Advisor is an open-source project built to empower students and professionals through responsible, ethical, and inclusive AI-driven career guidance.

We are committed to providing a **safe, welcoming, and harassment-free environment** for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of background, experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, nationality, race, religion, or technical expertise.

Participation in this project should be respectful, collaborative, and focused on building technology that genuinely helps people.

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of positive behavior include:
- Being respectful, patient, and considerate in discussions
- Offering constructive feedback and thoughtful suggestions
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Collaborating openly and professionally
- Accepting feedback and learning from mistakes
- Keeping discussions focused on improving the project

### Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, spamming, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent
- Misusing AI features to promote misinformation or unethical guidance

---

## Responsible AI & Career Guidance

Because this project operates in the **career and education domain** and uses AI:

- Do **not** present AI-generated output as guaranteed career outcomes
- Avoid misleading, exaggerated, or harmful career advice
- Clearly distinguish informational guidance from professional counseling
- Respect user privacy and avoid exposing personal data
- Ensure AI usage aligns with ethical and explainable practices

All contributions should prioritize **user trust, transparency, and fairness**.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repositories
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Code reviews and comments
- Documentation and design discussions
- Community interactions related to AI Career Advisor

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Actions may include:
- Requesting changes or clarification
- Issuing warnings
- Temporarily restricting participation
- Permanently removing contributors for severe or repeated violations

All enforcement decisions will be made fairly and in the interest of maintaining a healthy community.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it responsibly.

You may:
- Open a GitHub issue labeled **Code of Conduct**
- Contact the maintainers privately (if contact information is available)

Reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled with care and respect.
